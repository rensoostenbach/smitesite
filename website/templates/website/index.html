View some information on the God classes below, and click on the god class to go to the builds!

Hunters

Hunters are known to be very powerful in the lategame, and their builds will be centered around that.

Mages

Mages need to do a lot of damage throughout the whole game, which is reflected in their builds.

Guardians

Guardians need to have high defense stats, as well as provide support for the teammates.

Warriors

Warriors need to have a lot of defense, so they can initiate a fight, or help their own team.

Assassins

Assassins need to have a lot of power and penetration, so they can quickly take someone out of the fight.
